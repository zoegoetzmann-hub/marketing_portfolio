About Me

About Me

Placing genuine art in the spaces where life actually happens is something I have been living, not just marketing, for seven years.

At Cosimo Art I built the hospitality partnerships from scratch: art'otel London Hoxton, Renegade Urban Winery, University of Brighton. I curated the shows, wrote the copy, ran the socials and measured what landed. The result was a platform trusted by 1,000+ artists and award-winning enough to earn Deutsche Bank recognition.

Alongside that, I independently curated gallery exhibitions across London — always at the intersection of art and hospitality venues. I know what it takes to make a venue say yes, and what makes an artist feel genuinely supported.

I bring an instinct for clear, conversion-minded copy — honed at Cosimo Art — combined with a genuine network inside London's emerging art scene and a track record of building hospitality and institutional partnerships from the ground up.

Brand & Partnerships

Head of Partnerships & Curation — Cosimo Art

Aug 2023 – May 2025

Cosimo is a London-based art marketplace and SaaS platform for emerging artists. I joined to own brand, content and partnerships from the ground up.

Managed all digital content, copywriting and social media strategy for a 1,000+ artist platform via Hootsuite and Google Analytics — daily posts, captions, product copy and email comms.
Created and led the Guest Curator Picks editorial series, liaising with art world professionals including Verity Babbs (Guardian, Hyperallergic, Artnet) and Jo McLaughlin (Lead Curator, Acrylicize). Produced interview forms, social content and website editorial for each series.
Built and managed hospitality partnerships with art'otel London Hoxton, Renegade Urban Winery and University of Brighton. The art'otel partnership led directly to Selfsame (Jan 2026), reviewed by Amelia Holdsworth in Made in Bed (Sotheby's): "To thine own (her) self be true… Goetzmann's curatorial approach is notably restrained. Rather than imposing a dominant framework, the exhibition allows relationships between works to emerge organically."
Organised the inaugural Cosimo Art Emerging Art Prize end-to-end. Team awarded Deutsche Bank Awards for Creative Entrepreneurs (2023), GP Bullhound Award and Digital Innovation in Art Award (2022).

Exhibition Curation · Hotel Partnership

art'otel London Hoxton — Selfsame & Renaissance 25

Jan 2025 & Jan 2026

Two back-to-back exhibitions at art'otel London Hoxton, a design-led boutique hotel in Shoreditch where art is the brand — demonstrating the intersection of hospitality and art.

Selfsame (Jan 2026) — end-to-end curation: artist selection, wall text, PR, social content and opening event. Reviewed by Amelia Holdsworth for Made in Bed (Sotheby's).
Renaissance 25 — Cosimo Art × art'otel (Jan 2025) — partnership exhibition bridging the Cosimo artist community with the hotel's audience of art-curious guests.
Spoke on the panel "Art and Tech: A New Renaissance?" at art'otel London Hoxton, as part of Renaissance 25 (supported by Vieunite). Panellists included Jasmina Stevanov (AI/VR artist researcher), David Bull (Director, Hang My Art), Gretchen Andrew (artist, ex-Intuit & Google) and John Sewell (Cosimo Art).

Creative Producer — Spirit Now London

2022

Spirit Now London is a philanthropic art collector community. As freelance Creative Producer I shaped how the brand sounded and looked across Instagram — the primary channel for reaching art-curious, high-intent audiences.

Wrote captions, sourced and adapted visual assets and maintained a consistent brand voice across all content.
Covered high-profile events: Soheila Sokhanvari: Rebel Rebel at the Barbican and Frieze Masters — translating gallery experiences into accessible, engaging content.
Supported event communications and community engagement for collector-focused programming.
